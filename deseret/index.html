The Deseret Alphabet Portal

Do you like to study the history of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints? Do you like to study language or alternate alphabets? Did you know that Brigham Young, second president of the church, proposed an alternate alphabet for the English language? It was called The Deseret Alphabet and it is a hobby of mine. This website is a place where you can learn more about this fascinating aspect of church history. You can learn why this alternate alphabet was created and what happened to it, see pictures of books that were printed in it, and even learn to read and write in it yourself. You can even download and read a scanned copy of the entire Book of Mormon that was printed in the Deseret Alphabet.

This site is for hobbyist of the Deseret Alphabet, those who want to learn to read and write using the Deseret Alphabet, those who are currious about it's history, or just anyone that wants to know what is that funny writting that is frequently attributed to "the Mormons" and learn more about it.

There is a FaceBook group dedicated to the Deseret Alphabet where a lot of us fans hang out. Anyone that is interested in the Deseret Alphabet is welcome to join. It is a private group to avoid spam, so to join just click on the link requesting membership and be sure to answer all of the questions.

As more and more individuals become interested in the Deseret Alphabet, they will publish their work on the internet. This site is also meant to be a reseource for finding these other useful sites that contain information that is considered to be of worth to fans of the Deseret Alphabet. This site aims to make it easier to find resources, without having to weed through the many sites that will show up on a search engine, due to the general wording of Alphabet. The word Deseret however, is rather unique and can sometimes be used as an easy way to find more information. The links to other resources on this site are periodically checked and keep updated, as sites come and go. If you have a resource related to the Deseret Alphabet that you feel would be of worth to other fans and would like a link to it from this site, just send me a message.

The general public is also being introduced to the Deseret Alphabet as various sites publish articles on this “oddity” and strange part of “Mormon” history. Because of this more and more people are beginning to look up the Deseret Alphabet to find more information about it, and possibly learn to read and write in it themselves. This site aims to provide enough information for those users as a portal, or launching pad, for finding other resources about the Deseret Alphabet. Included on this site are scanned images of the first two Readers of the Deseret Alphabet, and part of the third book that was printed. Also included on this site are instructions on learning to read and write in the Deseret Alphabet that was created by other people that are skilled in language and writing who happen to also find the Deseret Alphabet interesting.

Some items that can be found on this site include scanned images of:

  • Deseret First Book
  • Deseret Second Book
  • Book of Mormon Part 1

And:

  • Triple Combination by John H. Jenkins
  • Complete-Guide-to-Reading-and-Writing-the-Deseret-Alphabet
  • Huneybee Font

This site, the Deseret Alphabet Portal, aims to be a long lasting and reliable source for information on the Deseret Alphabet for many years to come.

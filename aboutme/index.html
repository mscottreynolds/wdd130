Hello. My name is Scott. I was born in Apple Valley, California, but grew up in Salt Lake City, Utah. When I was married I lived in both Utah and Arizona. I now have been living in Herriman, Utah for the last 7 years.

I just finished PathwayConnect and this is my first semester at BYU-Idaho. My goal is to get a bachelors degree in Applied Technology.

A few years ago, I became disabled and now suffer from severe chronic-pain. While my life has completely changed because of the chronic pain and the associated trials, in a way it has been a blessing because it has brought me so much closer to God, my Heavenly Father and His son Jesus Christ. Heavenly Father is a God of miracles! He loves me (and you), and has been with me this whole time. I love Him and would not have survived without Him. While I still suffer from the trials of chronic pain, I am excited and look forward to a new life that has started with BYU-Pathway Worldwide.
